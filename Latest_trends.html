Latest trends of autumn 2024

"Fashion is very important. It is life-enhancing and, like everything that gives pleasure, it is worth doing well. Vivienne Westwood"

Minimalism

Minimalism is a classic of the genre. And this style never goes out of trend in all seasons. If you have autumn images in the style of minimalism, then you can consider yourself stylish.

Burgundy color

It will hardly surprise anyone that it was the burgundy color that took over all the catwalks at the fall-winter 2024 shows, especially since it appeared at Gucci and Bottega Veneta in the previous season. This shade will easily fit into any wardrobe: it combines with almost any color.

Fringle

Fringe has been a trend for several seasons now. Designers use it in their collections on various items of clothing and accessories.

Material suede

Suede is back in trend! For a long time, suede remained on the list of anti-trends. Things made from it were considered irrelevant, fashionistas preferred leather. However, not long ago this material returned to the catwalks, and then to the shelves of boutiques.
